What does ICO stand for?

How will the ICO work?

On February 8, 2018 at 12:00 PM PST (20:00 UTC), our website (http://www.kwhcoin.com) will display the link to fill out your personal contact information in a KYC (Know Your Customer) process. You will then be directed to the page with the address where you can send your contributions in ETH.

When will the ICO start and when will it end?

The ICO will begin at 12:00 PM PST (20:00 UTC) on February 8, 2018 and end at 12:00 PM PST (20:00 UTC) on March 8, 2018, or when all of the sale tokens have been sold, whichever is earlier.

How do I see my tokens in my wallet?

You need to add them as a custom token to your wallet. Here are the instructions if you are using MyEtherWallet.

  • Go to MyEtherWallet.com and click 'View Wallet Info'
  • Click the 'Add Custom Token' button
  • You should be prompted to enter 3 pieces of information, the Address, Token Symbol, and Decimals.
  • Enter the following pieces of information into the appropriate fields:
    • Enter the Token Address provided to you at the time of participating in the ICO in the 'Address' field
    • Enter KWH for in the 'Token Symbol' field
    • Enter 18 for the 'Decimals' field
